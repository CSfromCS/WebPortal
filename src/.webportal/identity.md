  Identity

  24-year-old Filipino software engineer at Microsoft Azure (Melbourne). Summa Cum Laude, Rank 1, Ateneo de Manila.
  Builds infrastructure at scale. Builds community on the ground. Emotionally direct, warm, structured but never stiff.
   The kind of person who talks about feelings mid-strategy-conversation and finds it funny, not awkward.

  Theme

  "Builder Who Bridges" — someone who builds things that work, for systems and for people. Not an achievement wall. Not
   a generic dev portfolio. A site that sounds and feels like a real person who happens to be technically excellent.

  Vibe

  Bold, warm, alive. Confident without performing confidence. Fun without trying to be quirky. Clean structure (he's a
  systems thinker) with human warmth (he's chalant — emotionally present and direct). Think: the energy of someone who
  commits to their choices fully and doesn't hedge.

  Color Palette

  - Background: Warm off-white / cream (#FAF7F2 or similar) — not stark white, not cold
  - Text: Deep warm charcoal (#2C2825) — not pure black
  - Primary accent: A bold, saturated green — alive, fresh, confident. Not muted sage, not neon. Think lush, tropical,
  with energy. (#2D8C4E range or deeper — adjust to taste)
  - Secondary accent: Warm gold or amber (#C4883A) for subtle highlights, hover states, tags
  - Avoid: Cold grays, Microsoft blue, pure black, neon, pastels

  Content Principles

  - Lead with what he cares about (building, bridging, community), not credentials
  - Credentials support the story — they don't lead it
  - Writing should sound like him: direct, warm, structured-casual, no corporate filler
  - Include the Filipino thread explicitly — migration, PinoyTech, community work
  - The "About" reads like explaining yourself over coffee, not a LinkedIn summary

  Structure (suggested)

  - Hero: One-liner + short intro that communicates the person, not the resume
  - Work/Projects: Craft and impact, not a job listing
  - Community: PinoyTech, CODE, mentoring — this is a differentiator, not a footnote
  - About: Identity, background, what drives him
  - Links: GitHub, LinkedIn, contact

  What This Site Is NOT

  - A cold achievement showcase
  - A dark-mode developer template
  - A corporate Microsoft employee page
  - Minimalist-for-minimalism's-sake
  - Trying to look older or more "serious" than he is
